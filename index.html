Trusted Mobile Auto Repair Since 1998

Cormack Auto Repair Services is a family-run business providing reliable and efficient mobile vehicle service with fair pricing. With 25 years of experience in mobile servicing, breakdown/light vehicle towing and recovery, we understand the importance of professional, courteous service whether at the roadside, home or workplace.

We assume our cars should operate correctly when travelling from A to B, but without routine maintenance and servicing, vehicle failure becomes more likely. Our mobile services help prevent these issues with convenient, professional care.

Manufacturer Warranty Protection

EU Block Exemption Regulations allow us to service your vehicle without invalidating manufacturer warranties when using appropriate quality parts.

Wide Coverage Area

We serve Fife, Lothians, Central, Perth, and Tayside regions with our mobile repair services.
